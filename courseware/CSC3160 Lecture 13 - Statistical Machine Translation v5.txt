                                            Syntactic divergences
      • Word order




      • Head-initial vs. Head-final: Word order
            • Head-initial(En): read books (Head:V +O)
            • Head-final(Jp):        本を読む            (O + Head:V)

      • Head-marking vs. dependent-marking
            • Head-marking (En): He speaks (N + Vs)
            • Dependent-marking(Jp) : 彼が 話す (Nが + V)
